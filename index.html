Abbot Initiative in

Mathematics

October 3, 2021

The Abbot Initiative in Mathematics (AIM) serves to empower young people of underrepresented genders in STEM through a one-day celebration of mathematics—endorsed and supported by Phillips Academy and the Abbot Academy Fund.

Event

AIM 2021 will be a virtual one-day event including a competition, speaker event, and community bonding activities for young mathematicians.

Click on each panel for more details about each section.

Contest

The competition aspect of AIM will consist of two rounds. Each round will be split into different sections depending on the participant's grade level.

  • Speed Round
    (30 Minutes, 20 Questions)
  • Challenge Round
    (25 Minutes, 10 Questions)

Rounds will be proctored by Phillips Academy students.
